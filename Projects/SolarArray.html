Solar Array

As the electrical director of PrISUm, I lead development of the array for the solar car, a rather important part of the project. I lead manufacture of the silicon array pictured here on Penumbra, and lead design and devlopment for the experimental array going on our next solar car. I learned a lot about semiconductor physics, as well as solar energy in general, as Iowa State only gets into high level solar cells junior/senior year, and I was thrown in headfirst as a freshman. I learned various diodes and protections against the cells, as well as learning how to design a solar system as a whole, from power in, to the boost to a usable voltage, to storage. Various solar cells of experimental chemistries were used, as well as solar design resources ranging from professional companies, to hobby equipment found on campus.
