Battery Protection System (BPS)

As one of the hardware designers on PrISUm, I helped design parts of the battery protection system that is used inside Penumbra, and parts of the system inside the current car, Eliana. The BPS monitors a variety of safety factors on the lithium ion batteries inside the car, and ensures that the car is always operating in a safe condition. I helped design some of the signal circuitry on the board, as well as lead manfuaturing and assembly of the battery pack as a whole in Australia. I learned quite a bit about protection circuity, as it was critical that this board did not fail, as well as electrical design. Altium designer, an industry standard PCB and schematic software was used in the creation and manufacture of the board.
