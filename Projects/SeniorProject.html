Senior Design

High Speed Magnetic Field Generator

For my senior project at Iowa State, I am designing a High Speed Magnetic Field Generator. In my group, I am the technology lead, which involves helping design the circuit and managing all the software/hardware required to make the circuit a physical working model. This will involve managing the webpage, and helping with circuit creation and design through PSPICE and Altium Designer. I will learn quite a bit about project management, as well as further my skills in high frequency circuit design, as the functional circuit will have 500 gauss generated with a rapid rise time, the faster the better.
