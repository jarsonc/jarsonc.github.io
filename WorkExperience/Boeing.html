Boeing

At Boeing, I worked as an engineer with the Training and Professional Services division, working in Simulator Support, supporting new programs such as the 777X. I worked on automating processes converting flight ready software to simulator specific software, since the configruations of a plane and a simluator will be different. For this, I used scripting in python to automate much of the heavy work. In addition to these tasks, I also worked on networking inside the 777X, linking all the flight systems together. I used their Avionics Integration Lab and their engineering cab, which simluates an entire plane system in a laboratory or simlulator to test all the connections, and troubleshoot whatever problems came up.
