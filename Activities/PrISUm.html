PrISUm Solar Car

PrISUm takes up most of my time on campus. PrISUm is an interdisciplinary organization with the mission to build a practical solar car that changes the paradigm of transportation. In doing so, we strive to inspire future generations to pursue their passions through education. On this team, I am the incoming electrical director, aftre having contributed as a project manager and part of the World Solar Challenge race crew. I specialize in Printed Circuit Board (PCB) design and troubleshooting, having personally be invovled with at least half the car's current electronics. I've also rewired a great deal of the car. My main job at this time is to manage various projects on the team, make sure that groups have resources necessary to finish their projects, and helping out whenever I'm reqested to.
